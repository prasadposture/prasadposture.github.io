Research Output

Journal of Cosmology and Astroparticle Physics  ·  IOP Publishing  ·  2026
CosmoUiT: a vision transformer-UNet hybrid for fast and accurate emulation of 21-cm maps from the epoch of reionization
Prasad Rajesh Posture, Yashrajsinh Mahida, Suman Majumdar, Leon Noble
The observation of redshifted 21-cm signals from the intergalactic medium will probe the epoch of reionization with exceptional precision. Creating multiple simulations to predict this signal is computationally expensive. CosmoUiT introduces a UNet-integrated vision transformer-based architecture designed to emulate 3D cubes of 21-cm signals from dark matter density fields and reionization parameters. The model employs transformer self-attention for long-range dependencies alongside convolutional layers for small-scale variations, with training conditioned on reionization parameters enabling accurate predictions across different parameter sets. Validated through statistical comparisons (bubble size distributions, power spectra) and morphological feature analysis, demonstrating high-accuracy emulation at both large and small scales.
